7. Flight Booking Website

The flight booking website you developed is a user-friendly platform that simplifies the process of searching, comparing, and booking flights. It features an intuitive interface where travelers can easily enter their departure and arrival locations, travel dates, and passenger details to find suitable options. Advanced filtering allows users to sort results by price, duration, and airline, ensuring quick access to the best choices. The site integrates with multiple Global Distribution Systems (GDS) for real-time availability and pricing, while also offering ancillary services like seat selection and baggage options. With a secure payment gateway and automated ticketing, the website provides a seamless booking experience that enhances customer satisfaction throughout their travel planning journey.
